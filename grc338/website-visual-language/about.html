About Us

At Crop to Cart, we have cultivated a passion for connecting communities with the bounty of our region's rich and diverse agricultural landscape. Our mission is simple yet profound: to bring the freshest, most unique produce directly from local farms to your doorstep. With a commitment to supporting the hardworking farmers of the central coast, we've curated an exquisite selection of fruits, vegetables, and artisanal goods that showcase the region's agricultural brilliance.

Discover the essence of Carmel's terroir through our user-friendly website, where you can effortlessly browse, select, and order your favorite items. From the verdant fields of Salinas Valley to the sun-kissed orchards of Monterey County, Crop to Cart ensures that each product is carefully sourced for its quality, flavor, and sustainable practices. Join us in savoring the seasonality of California's central coast, one delectable bite at a time.

Crop to Cart– where the fields meet your doorstep, and every carton is a celebration of freshness, flavor, and community.
